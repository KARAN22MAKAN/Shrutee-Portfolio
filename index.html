Brief N° 04 — Brand Strategy

OWND! Concept Project

Brand / Organisation
OWND! — positioned under Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Limited (ABFRL)
Objective
Build a brand for a generation that wants confident, effortless style without chasing trends or overpaying.
My Role
Identifying the target audience for the brand concept.
Strategy & Narrative
Positioned the brand as "not a label, a verb" — fashion that fits real moments: campus, streets, workdays, weekends — collections designed to move at the pace of the people wearing them.

Brief N° 05 — Content Strategy

Aretto — 30-Day Content Calendar Concept Project

Brand
Aretto — kids' footwear
Objective
Build awareness for adjustable, "grows with your child" footwear and move parents from problem-awareness to trust to purchase consideration.
Approach
Pillar-based calendar (Product Awareness, Problem-Solution, Product Education, Social Proof, Engagement, Brand Trust) mapped to specific formats — video, carousel, reel, static — with a rationale for each post.
Platforms
Conceptual — no live posting took place; formats were designed for Instagram-style distribution (reel, carousel, static).

Brief N° 06 — Creative Design

Aretto — "From Cartoon to Cool" Part of Brief N° 05

Relationship
This creative set was produced as part of the Aretto content calendar project above — the executed asset for the calendar's "Product Showcase by Shoe Colors" post concept.
Concept
A product-showcase creative series pairing familiar cartoon/character themes with the shoe design, tying the "from cartoon to cool" growth narrative to the product's own "grows with your child" idea.
Deliverables
Set of themed product creatives styled as social carousel cards.
